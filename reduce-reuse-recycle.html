Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle

Reduce

  • Filtered water is provided to discourage the use of disposable water bottles.
  • Converting many processes to be electronic, to reduce the printing and wasting of paper.
  • Memos are distributed via email with only one master flier posted, instead of papers to each employee.
  • Equipment and supplies are shared between some departments, rather than per employee.
  • Documents, such as handbooks, are created with limited spacing and margins to reduce pages per print job.

Reuse

  • We don’t use disposable cups, instead we use our BPA Free Anton Sport promotional cups which can be washed and reused.
  • We receive a large number of shipments from vendors every day. We reuse the boxes both internally and by re-purposing them for shipping customer orders. We also include a recycled decal tracking the box’s travels.
  • We bale and send inbound cartons from the vendors to a recycle and we reuse our cartons internally, including those that are sent to our retail stores.
  • Old or broken electronics are kept for parts, so we can replace equipment rather than purchase new ones.
  • Scrap paper is collected to be reused for internal documents or scratch paper.

Recycle

  • White and colored paper
  • Converting many processes to be electronic, to reduce the printing and wasting of paper.
  • Corrugate
  • Aluminum Cans
  • Cartons unable to travel further
